About us

The world's population is growing very rapidly, yet the globe is getting smaller through social networking and ICT. Here at GreenRas, we believe that by closing the gap between agriculture and social networking (ICT), we can make food production from urban and small-scale farming sufficient and sustainable even with the rapidly growing population. That is why we are working hard every day to deliver a social trading platform to individuals from all socio-economic climates to grow and sell nutritional produce with minimal expenses.
